We are The Sneaker Club

We are Sneaker Club; Also known as SC for short. We currently reside in Providence, Rhode Island. We were first established in the year 2018. When we first opened we were nothing but a small shop in the hood of the 401. After a few years of working hard and grinding, our shop expanded. Our customer base grew vastly and as so did our inventory. We went from classic releases to more rare and hyped pieces. As a sneaker shop, we want to provide our customers the best service and we want to be the plug for every sneakerhead out there.

Sneaker Club Rewards

We work with the large name brand companies, Jordan, Nike, and Adidas. We have partnered with them to bring the latest releases to our customers. We know how much of a demand it is for every sneakerhead to get the newest and rarest drops.
